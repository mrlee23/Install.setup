* Overview
  :PROPERTIES: 
  :CUSTOM_ID: overview
  :LANG: en
  :END:      

Change the login shell.

** Characteristic
  :PROPERTIES: 
  :LANG: en
  :END:    

- Changes the user's login shell.
- Normal users can only change their login shell.
- The superuser can change the login shell of all users.
